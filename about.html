About Adelina Cakes

Raw Delight Cakes was born from a love for simple, natural ingredients and a desire to create desserts that nourish both body and soul. Every cake is handmade with care, using only high-quality plant-based ingredients.

I believe that sweets can be both delicious and mindful. That’s why all my cakes are vegan, gluten-free, and free from refined sugar — created to support a balanced and joyful lifestyle.

Each cake tells its own story, inspired by nature, seasons, and honest flavors. Whether it’s a celebration or a quiet moment for yourself, my goal is to make it a little more special.
